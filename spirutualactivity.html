Vedic - Hindu Spirituality & Mysticism quotations page is one of a series of seven pages on our site that consider the extensive! range of deep! agreement about important aspects of spirituality and spiritual mysticism between several major World Religions.

Acharya is the first and the only DarshanakAra to have provided a commentary on vedic sUktas. He did this to bring out the spiritual aspect of the Vedas and to underscore the point that all Vedic verses and words are basically the names of the Lord. He also wanted to indicate that a true Vedantin cannot and should not ignore the Vedas.
